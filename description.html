Max: The "Happy Pill"

Came from St. Louis, Missouri, Max brings happiness to sad-faced people. His affectionate disposition makes him a wonderful family pet and therapy dog.

02

An intelligent dog

Max is highly intelligent and eager to please individuals. With his intelligence, coupled with his willingness to learn, makes him highly trainable for various tasks and commands.

03

Playful and full of energy

This good boy loves activities such as swimming, playing fetch, and going on long walks or runs with their owners. Max's love for water can be attributed to his water-repellent coat and his history as hunting companions.
